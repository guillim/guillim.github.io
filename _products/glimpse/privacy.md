  <h1 style="font-size: 2em; margin-bottom: 0.5em;">Privacy Policy</h1>

  <p><strong>Guillaume Lancrenon</strong> respects your privacy.</p>

  <p>We try to collect only information that we really need, and minimise the amount of information we store.</p>

  <p>Glimpse is a desktop application that runs locally on your computer. The software does not have any "cloud" features. All processing happens entirely on-device.</p>

  <p>Glimpse does not transfer any of your data to a web service or similar.</p>

  <p>You do not need to trust us with your data, because we don't have your data.</p>

  <p>However, we do collect a minimal amount of statistical information, as described below.</p>

  <h2 style="font-size: 1.35em; margin-top: 1.5em;">This website</h2>

  <p>We store full web server access and error logs for a limited time. Additionally, we may store logs about software downloads.</p>

  <p>These logs may include IP addresses.</p>

  <h2 style="font-size: 1.35em; margin-top: 1.5em;">Glimpse app</h2>

  <p>Glimpse may use a web service to check for updates. When checking for updates, Glimpse may send information such as:</p>

  <ul>
    <li>App version number</li>
    <li>macOS version</li>
    <li>Computer model</li>
    <li>Language preference</li>
  </ul>

  <p>We use this information to understand how many users we have and what macOS versions to support.</p>

  <p>You can disable automatic update checking in Glimpse preferences if available.</p>

  <h2 style="font-size: 1.35em; margin-top: 1.5em;">Contact</h2>

  <p>If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact <a href="mailto:anchor.guillaume+glimpse@gmail.com">anchor.guillaume+glimpse@gmail.com</a>.</p>
